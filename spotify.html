Visual Tools is a collection of Figma plugins that support designer in their daily workflows and craft. From smoothing gradients, creating radial interfaces to creating smooth shadows, these tools allow designers to focus on hitting the nail rather thinking about how to hold the hammer.
My role: Solo UX design + research
Timeline: June - August 2022
Tools: Figma, Notion, octopus.do

Problem

Music streaming isolates the user.
The scarcity and finiteness of physical mediums force listeners to treat them as such: un-expendable pieces of media to be shared and preserved. In turn, these limitations, imposed by older mediums of music consumption (vinyl, CDs, iTunes, etc,), fostered a sense of community that music streaming services fail to replicate.
